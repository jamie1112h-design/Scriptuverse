Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 12, 2026

1. Who We Are

Scriptuverse is a business registered in Ontario, Canada. Our website is located at scriptuverse.com.

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: scriptuversesupport@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address – collected when you sign in, used to authenticate your account via a one–time sign–in link.
  • Faith tradition and Bible version – collected once, at sign–up, to anchor the scripture–based guidance you receive across Counsel, Refuge, and any future instrument.
  • Session content – what you write when using an instrument, including the situation, concern, or question you bring to Counsel or Refuge.

Information collected automatically during use

  • Usage data – which instrument you use, and whether a session used one of your free trial sessions or your active subscription.
  • We do not collect your name, age, postal address, phone number, or any government–issued identification.

Payment information

Payments are processed exclusively by Stripe, Inc. We do not receive, store, or have access to your credit card number or full payment details. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data and is available at stripe.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To sign you in and authenticate your session.
  • To generate scripture–based guidance responses anchored to your stated tradition and Bible version.
  • To track your free trial usage and subscription status.
  • We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell your data to any third party.

We will not provide, sell, or otherwise share your email address, your session content, or any information linked to them with any third party, under any circumstances, except as described in Section 6 below.

4. A Note on Sensitive Conversations

Scriptuverse's Refuge instrument, and at times Counsel, may involve conversations touching on grief, fear, or emotional distress. If a session contains language suggesting a risk of self–harm or crisis, our system records that a crisis–related event occurred, independent of how the conversation itself proceeds – this record exists for continuity and safety purposes, not to interrupt or end the conversation. Scriptuverse does not contact emergency services on your behalf and is not a substitute for professional mental health care, pastoral counseling, or emergency services.

If you are in crisis or believe you may harm yourself, please contact your local emergency number, a crisis line such as 988 (United States) or Talk Suicide Canada at 1–833–456–4566, or a trusted person in your life directly. If you are located outside the United States or Canada, a directory of crisis lines by country is available at findahelpline.com.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a Supabase database. We use industry–standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and role–based access controls.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that exposes your email address or session content, we will notify you as required under applicable law.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address or session content with any third party for their own purposes. We use a small number of infrastructure providers solely to operate Scriptuverse itself – for example, to process payments, host the website, and deliver the service – and only to the extent necessary to provide you with the service. If you have any question about how your information is handled, contact us at scriptuversesupport@gmail.com.

7. Age Requirement

Scriptuverse is intended for users 18 years of age or older, given the nature of the guidance offered and the possibility that sessions may touch on grief, fear, or personal crisis. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, please contact us at scriptuversesupport@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent to the collection or use of your information, where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at scriptuversesupport@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Our data practices comply with applicable Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and we extend these rights to all users globally.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

Scriptuverse uses browser cookies and local session storage solely to keep you signed in between visits. No advertising cookies or third–party tracking cookies are used. You may clear cookies at any time through your browser settings; if you do, you will simply need to sign in again with your email address to receive a new one–time sign–in link.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of Scriptuverse after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
